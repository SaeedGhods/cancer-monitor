Nutritional Strategy

Reduce Blood Sugar Through Diet

High blood sugar levels can fuel cancer growth. By making strategic dietary changes, you can reduce your glucose levels and create an environment less favorable for cancer development.

Key Dietary Strategies

  • Reduce refined carbohydrates and sugars
  • Increase fiber-rich foods and vegetables
  • Choose low-glycemic index foods
  • Incorporate healthy fats and lean proteins
  • Practice intermittent fasting or time-restricted eating
